Ingredients

  • Frozen fruit of your choice
  • 1.5 bananas
  • Scoop of soy bean butter
  • 1/2 tsp kale powder
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • Soy milk

Steps

  1. Add ingredients in order to your bullet style blender, then blend and serve
  2. Enjoy!
