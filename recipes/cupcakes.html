Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened soy milk (or use another non-dairy milk)
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 3/4 cups cake flour*
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup melted vegan butter or neutral oil
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 Recipe Vegan Buttercream Frosting

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix wet ingredients
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, then mix well
  4. Fill 24 cupcake liners to half full
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes until done
  6. Let cool completely, then frost as desired
