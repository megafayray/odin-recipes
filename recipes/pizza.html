Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Warm water
  • Cornmeal
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Mix water, sugar, instant yeast together and let sit 5 minutes until frothy
  3. Add remaining ingredients, mix, then knead until smooth
    4. \
  4. Sprinkle counter with extra cornmeal, then roll dough into a circle the size of your pizza pan
  5. Top with sauce and other toppings, then bake for 12 minutes until pizza is done
